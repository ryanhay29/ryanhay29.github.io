About me

Hi, I'm Ryan!

I'm driven by a curiosity for how systems work end to end, from hardware to software.

On collaborative coursework and club projects, I've played a key role in designing solutions for a wide range of engineering problems including a front-end loader bucket for excavating lunar regolith, a guitar fret-pressing mechanism, a bench-fence hybrid to prevent child drowning, and a pillow designed for glasses-wearers.

Outside of class, I play guitar, draw, and animate. I also have a background in technical theatre, where I stage managed and co-designed sets and award-winning lighting.
